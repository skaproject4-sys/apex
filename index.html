APEX AQUALIS

Overview

APEX Group Housing at Sector ETA II, Greater Noida — where water meets light and living meets serenity. AQUALIS offers 3 BHK, 4 BHK, and 5 BHK townhouses across new residential towers with a vast central landscape and state-of-the-art club amenities.

13.03 acres of master-planned development with 592 units in new towers, saleable area of approx. 15.86 lakh sq.ft. Tower A & B rise to 35 floors with Social Club; Tower C to 31 floors with Sports Club. Grand drop-off entrances, double-height lobbies, and barrier-free accessibility with vehicular movement restricted to entry and exit only.

Private entrance foyers, obstruction-free formal areas, and spacious living with flexible drawing and dining. Every residence is designed for luxury and harmony with nature — flow into the life of serenity.

About APEX Group

Our Story

APEX Group is a trusted name in North India's real estate sector, known for creating world-class residential and commercial spaces. Built on the values of trust, transparency, and quality, the group has delivered landmark projects that reflect modern design, innovation, and lifestyle excellence.

With a strong focus on timely delivery, sustainable development, and customer satisfaction, APEX Group has earned the trust of thousands of families. Every project is thoughtfully planned to offer not just homes, but complete living experiences with modern amenities and future-ready infrastructure.

Backed by a team of professionals and a vision to redefine urban living, APEX Group continues to expand its footprint across NCR and beyond. Its mission is simple – to enrich lifestyles by building spaces that combine comfort, community, and contemporary design.
